Welcome to Sierra Leone!

Sierra Leone is a beautiful country, full of naturally breathtaking environments, from the stunning beaches to its lush mountains and islands. This is APP is meant as a guide for people planning trips to sierra leone, it is meant to help you find where to stay, where to eat, places to see and where relax and have fun. Discover Salone is not just meant for tourists, it was also designed with the locals at heart, with various categories include Accommodation, Food, Events and Nightlife, everyone has access to the best that Sierra Leone (Salone) has to offer as well where. Right now the data is been update every 24 hour and a live stream of events. With time we would also add a functionality that allow users to leave comments about locations and also rate them. Enjoy!
